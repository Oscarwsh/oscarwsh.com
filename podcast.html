The +2 Podcast

+2 Podcast is all about speedcubing and the culture around it. Hosted by Oscarwsh, some episodes include live conversation and viewer interaction. We talk competitions, cubing news, personal experiences, and ways to improve both your cubing and mindset. Whether you're listening live or later, this podcast is a place to think, laugh, and maybe even get a +2 along the way.

Where to Listen?

You can find it on Spotify, Apple Podcasts, and YouTube!
